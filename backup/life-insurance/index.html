Types of Life Insurance Coverage

Choose between term and permanent life insurance options based on your needs and budget

Term Life Insurance

$20-100/month

Temporary coverage for a specific period with affordable premiums

  • 10, 20, or 30-year terms
  • Level premium periods
  • Renewable options
  • Convertible to permanent
  • Pure death benefit protection

Whole Life Insurance

$200-800/month

Permanent coverage with cash value accumulation component

  • Lifetime protection
  • Cash value growth
  • Fixed premiums
  • Dividend potential
  • Tax-deferred savings

Universal Life Insurance

$100-500/month

Flexible permanent coverage with investment components

  • Adjustable premiums
  • Flexible death benefits
  • Investment options
  • Cash value accumulation
  • Potential for growth

Variable Life Insurance

$150-600/month

Investment-linked permanent coverage with market participation

  • Investment control
  • Variable returns
  • Tax advantages
  • Death benefit flexibility
  • Professional management options
