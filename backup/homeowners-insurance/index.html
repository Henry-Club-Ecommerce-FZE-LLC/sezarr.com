Homeowners Insurance Coverage Types

Understand the six standard coverage types that protect your home and belongings

Dwelling Coverage (Coverage A)

$200-400/year per $100K

Protection for your home's structure and attached components

  • Main dwelling structure
  • Built-in appliances
  • Attached garages
  • Interior fixtures and trim
  • Replacement cost basis

Personal Property (Coverage C)

50-70% of dwelling coverage

Protection for your belongings and personal possessions

  • Furniture and electronics
  • Clothing and jewelry
  • Sports equipment
  • Off-premises coverage
  • Replacement cost option

Liability Protection (Coverage E)

$100K-$1M+ limits

Legal protection for accidents on your property

  • Bodily injury liability
  • Property damage liability
  • Medical payments
  • Legal defense costs
  • Personal injury protection

Additional Living Expenses (Coverage D)

20-30% of dwelling coverage

Temporary housing costs during covered damage repairs

  • Hotel and rental costs
  • Restaurant meals
  • Storage fees
  • Pet boarding
  • Transportation expenses
